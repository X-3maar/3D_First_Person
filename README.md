# Pistols Arena 3D
a 3d shooter game made with godot where you try to find and shoot a target to reach the next level, it also has an online mode coming very soon.
## Controls
WASD or arrows to move around, space to jump, ctrl to toggle cursor lock, escape to pause, right click to aim and left click to shoot.
## Hints
For a better experience, do not read anything under this line before you give up passing a speicific level.
### Level 0
Just shoot the target.
### Level 1
The target is a bit further but you can still shoot it.
### Level 2
Jump on the platforms and reach to top, then shoot to open the door.
### Level 3
Snipe the moving target.
### Level 4
The hidden target is located in the back right of the room.
### Level 5
Get out of the maze and dfind the door, find the hidden target near the door, shoot it.
### Level 6
Snipe the target twice.
### Level 7
Snipe the 5 targets (Warning: do not shoot the 5th one if you're not close to the door)
### Level 8
Find the 2 targets on the first floor of the maze, find another 2 on the second floor then slide into the hole. (Warning: if you shoot the 5th one ad missed the door you will need to replay the game from level 1 again)
### Level 9
You can see the platforms in the mirror, jump until you reach the target.
### Level 10
The Final Boss will always dodge your bullet, but he moves in 3 constant positions. Expect his next move and aim exactly at the point he's gonna move to (you should snipe the target on his chest btw).
### Endless Mode
The difficulty increases with your score.
